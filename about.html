About HookSide

Our Story

HookSide began with a simple love for crocheting and the joy of creating handmade pieces that bring warmth, color, and personality into everyday life. What started as a hobby soon grew into a passion — and eventually into a small business that celebrates creativity, craftsmanship, and sustainable fashion.

Every item we offer is carefully handmade, stitched with patience, dedication, and a whole lot of heart. From bucket hats to bags, accessories, keychains, shrugs, and more — each piece is crafted to help you express your unique style.

Our Mission

To provide beautifully handcrafted crochet items that inspire self-expression, comfort, and confidence — while promoting slow fashion and mindful craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To become a trusted crochet brand known for quality, creativity, and heartfelt handmade pieces that customers can cherish and enjoy.

Meet the Maker

HookSide is proudly handmade in the Philippines. Behind every stitch is passion, creativity, and the desire to create pieces that make people smile. Thank you for supporting handmade!
